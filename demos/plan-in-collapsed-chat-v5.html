Gates are item-height rows at the same indent level as section items. Amber icon + amber text for active, green strikethrough for passed, grey for future. Reviewer shown as a small pill on the right. Gates are clearly "steps" — not structural elements.

B. Divider Tag

Gates are thin horizontal dividers with a centered pill/tag. Like an <hr> with a label. Extremely lightweight — they create visual separation between sections without demanding attention. Color coding (green/amber/grey) still differentiates state.

C. Left Border

Gates use a colored left border accent (like a blockquote) instead of a background fill. Same vertical weight as a work item, but the border stripe + amber color makes it distinct. Two lines: gate label + brief context. Reviewer as a right-aligned pill.
