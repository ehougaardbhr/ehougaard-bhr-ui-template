Plan v8: Two States

Plan Artifact — Two Card States

The plan card has two distinct modes. Proposal: AI presents a plan for human review before anything runs. Execution: plan is approved and running, with real-time progress. Review steps have a clear call-to-action when it's the user's turn.

What changed v8b

#1
No "gate" language
Replaced "review gate" with "review step" everywhere. "After Gate 2" becomes "Pending Sarah's review" — names the person, not the mechanism.
Language
#2
Continuous progress bar
Single smooth bar instead of per-item segments. Fills to 40% (4/10 done). Simpler and less noisy.
Visual
#3
No footer buttons during execution
Once the plan is running, Edit and Review buttons are gone. Footer shows progress only. The action lives on the review step itself.
Interaction
#4
Inline "Needs review" button on ready steps
When AI finishes its work and it's the user's turn, the review row gets a green "Needs review" button with hand icon. Green tint + pulse draws the eye. Unmistakable: "your turn."
New affordance
#5
"Needs Your Review" badge
Card badge changes from "Awaiting Review" to "Needs Your Review" — more direct, more personal. Makes clear the AI is paused on you.
Language
#6
"Pending [name]'s review" badges
Blocked sections show who they're waiting on by name. "Pending Sarah's review" instead of "After Gate 2." Human and specific.
Language
