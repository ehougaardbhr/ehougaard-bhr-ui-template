A. Checklist Card

Full plan card embedded in chat. Shows every action item as a checkbox with status. Richest information density — the user sees exactly what's in the plan without leaving the chat.

B. Section Progress Rows

C. Kanban Mini

Three-column kanban layout (To Do / In Progress / Done) showing items as mini cards. Gives immediate visual sense of workload distribution. Very recognizable pattern from project tools like Jira/Trello. Compact but information-rich.

D. Timeline / Steps

Vertical timeline showing plan sections as sequential phases. Emphasizes the order/progression of work. Dot states indicate which phase is active. Good for plans that have clear sequencing. Feels like a "journey" through the work.

E. Inline Flat List

No card at all — plan items render directly in the message flow as a styled list with colored status dots. Lightest weight, feels like the AI is just "saying" the plan items. No visual separation from the conversation. Easiest to scan quickly but hardest to interact with.
