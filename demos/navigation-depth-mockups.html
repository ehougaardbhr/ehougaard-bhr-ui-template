Recommendation Summary

Best for most users: Split View (#3) or Tab Bar (#5)

  • Context always visible without extra clicks
  • Familiar patterns (tabs from browsers, split view from IDEs)
  • Low cognitive load

Most lightweight: Floating Pill (#7) or Context Strip (#2)

  • Minimal UI footprint
  • One-click return to context
  • Good for larger workspace needs
